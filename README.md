An interactive, production-grade data science application designed to monitor customer retention, analyze demographic risk profiles, and track capital attrition across Eurozone retail banking markets. ## 📋 Project Architecture & Overview

Commercial entities operating under systemic retail banking conditions encounter liquidity volatility when asset bases churn out-of-system. This platform provides risk officers and macro-prudential regulators with real-time insight into asset flight risk using advanced data vectorization and diagnostic visualizations.

### Key Analytical Pillars
* **The Cross-Selling Paradox Matrix:** Visualizes a critical threshold where high-density product bundling accelerates relationship breakdown instead of cementing loyalty.
* **Mandated Categorical Clustering:** Segments portfolio records into strict, policy-compliant risk dimensions (Age Bands, Credit Risk Marks, and Tenure Lifecycles).
* **Capital Loss Exposure Engine:** Calculates live asset preservation indices and overall financial exposures using user-defined capital thresholds.

## 🚀 App Core Features

The dashboard operates via two distinct operational interfaces tailored to specific enterprise personas:

### 1. Operational Analytics Deck
Designed for internal risk management, portfolio analysts, and asset managers.
* **Dynamic Metric Deck:** Tracks overall portfolio bases, baseline churn percentages, high-value asset churn, and geographic risk markers simultaneously.
* **Interactive Filtering:** Slices and dices metrics instantly across sovereign territories (France, Germany, Spain), engagement statuses, and gender markers.
* **Live Audit Workspace Ledger:** A real-time, instantly searchable database allowing audit checks on specific customer profiles.

### 2. Government Stakeholder Summary
A dedicated view mode tailored specifically for state regulators, central banking supervisors, and compliance evaluators.
* **Macro Supervision Briefing Framework:** Condenses risk signals into systemic findings regarding age corridors and geographic exposure vectors.
* **Regulatory Directives:** Outlines actionable liquidity policy directives (e.g., targeted capital buffers and mandatory pre-sale product satisfaction checks).

## 🛠️ Technology Stack & Requirements

The data pipeline and front-end interface are built completely out of open-source Python optimization modules:
